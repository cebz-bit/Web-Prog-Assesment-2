Main Characters

Ri Shin: Ri Shin, formerly known as Shin, is the main protagonist of Kingdom. He is the leader of the powerfully famous specialist army unit, the Hi Shin Unit, and a General of the Qin Military.

Ei Sei: Ei Sei is the 31st King of the State of Qin. He was crowned at age 13, however, he did not become the official king until he was 22 of age in his Coming of Age Ceremony. His father was the previous king, Sou Jou, and his Bi Ki was formerly a first-class dancing girl from Zhao.

Ka Ryo Ten: She is the adoptive younger sister of Ri Shin, having grown close to him after years of fighting together as comrades-in-arms. She is also one of the last surviving descendants of the Kyuumei Tribe, a once powerful mountain tribe that was wiped out decades ago in a war against a young Yo Tan Wa and her army.

Kyou Kai: Kyou Kai is the second lieutenant of the Hi Shin Unit, a renowned specialist military unit, and a General of the Qin Military. She initially served as the unit’s primary Strategist during its time as a 100-Man unit but later left at the end of the Sanyou Campaign Arc, with Ka Ryo Ten succeeding her in that role.
